Who We Are

Sri Amman Modern Rice Mill is a leading rice mill located in Sivagiri-Erode, specializing in producing high-quality rice varieties for wholesalers, retailers, and households.

Our Mission

  • To produce clean, hygienic, and nutritious rice using advanced milling technology.
  • To provide consistent quality and timely delivery to all our customers.

Our Vision

To be one of the most trusted rice brands, delivering excellence from farm to plate.
